Business

Ways to help you and your business out

Interested in consulting for your business?

Do you own [or work for] a small business and feel ready to cut the slack? If there’s a project you always planned on, but had to keep pushing down the waiting list, or inefficiencies you feel are hindering your business’s growth, CUBE might be your solution.

Your Involvement

To ensure effective time expenditure and complete your goals [our goals, the goals] for your business, our small team of 4-5 consultants will try to meet with you at least once a week. Following your interest in a semester-long project, our team will meet with you in the following days to answer any questions and map out specifics.

Project Scope

Due to the nature of our consulting organization, our official start dates tend to be inflexible, as they fall around the University student’s semester schedule. While we begin our new projects every January and August, we have several consultants in our external relations team that work throughout the year to find potential clients. We’ll help you determine goals for your business and define a project scope that works for you.

If costs or financial uncertainties are concerning you, our official cost is free. However, to help us continue to grow as an organization and our consultants to build experience, we do provide every client a recommended donation amount. All donations are tax deductible as a 501(c)3 organization.
